Overview

ReaKt is a control software designed to optimize and simulate industrial fermentation processes. It goes beyond trial-and-error methods by combining a Deep Learning-based Digital Twin (LSTM) with a Model Predictive Control (MPC) to optimize inputs in real time.

Digital Twin

LSTM trained on historical batches to learn non-linear biological dynamics.

MPC Strategist

Optimizes sugar feed, aeration, temperature, and pH under constraints.

Your Data

Train the LSTM on your own dataset to adapt the process to your conditions.
