Overview

ReaKt is control software designed to optimize and simulate industrial fermentation processes. It goes beyond the trial-and-error approach by combining a Deep Learning (LSTM) Digital Twin with Model Predictive Control (MPC) to optimize inputs in real time.

Digital Twin

LSTM trained on historical batches to learn nonlinear biological dynamics.

MPC Strategist

Optimizes sugar feed, aeration, temperature, and pH under constraints.

Your Data

Train the LSTM on your own dataset to tailor the process.
