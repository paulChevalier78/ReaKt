Overview

ReaKt is a control software designed to optimize and simulate industrial fermentation processes. It moves beyond trial-and-error by combining a Deep Learning Digital Twin (LSTM) with Model Predictive Control (MPC) to optimize inputs in real-time.

Digital Twin

LSTM trained on historical batches to learn non-linear biological dynamics.

MPC Strategist

Optimizes Sugar Feed, Aeration, Temperature, pH under constraints.

Own Data

Train the LSTM on your own dataset to adapt to your process.

How It Works

  1. 1. Digital Twin (LSTM)

    Predicts Penicillin and Biomass concentrations ahead of time.

  2. 2. MPC Optimizer

    Solves a real-time optimization problem for future control actions.

  3. 3. Closed Loop

    Feeds optimal setpoints back to the process and adapts continuously.

  4. 4. Replay & Benchmark

    Analyze past batches and compare MPC vs historical performance.

Features & Interface

3D Digital Twin

Animated 3D bioreactor (Plotly) with volume, RPM, aeration bubbles, and color change based on biomass.

Real-time Monitoring

Live KPIs (Penicillin, Biomass, Volume) and control variables.

Interactive Replay

"DVR" mode to replay batches and inspect MPC decisions.

Process Charts

Plots of Sugar, Air, Acid, Base vs biological outputs.

Hardware Aware

Runs on CPU/GPU with efficient memory management.

Simple Setup

Train, save model + scalers, run Streamlit app with your data.
